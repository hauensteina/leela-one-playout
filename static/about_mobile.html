This is Leela Zero on 256 playouts, with some randomness
      in the first 25 moves. She is definitely pro strength, but
      also amusing, because she plays 3-4 or 3-3 on occasion.

      If you get tired of losing, play one of the other bots.
      Let Leela help you in a pinch.

      ---------

      and always plays the strongest move

      The other bots do not read
      and sometimes play moves that are debatable.
